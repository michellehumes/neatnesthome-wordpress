How to Choose the Right Pantry Organizers

Step 1: Measure Your Space

Before buying anything, measure your pantry shelves—height, width, and depth. The most common mistake is buying organizers that don't fit.

Step 2: Assess What You Store

  • Dry goods (pasta, rice, cereal): Airtight containers
  • Canned goods: Can racks or risers
  • Snacks and packets: Bins with handles
  • Spices: Tiered racks or drawer inserts
  • Oils and vinegars: Turntables (lazy susans)

Step 3: Start Small

Don't buy everything at once. Start with one category (like dry goods), organize it, live with it for a week, then move to the next area.
