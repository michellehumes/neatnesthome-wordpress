Final Tips for Small Closet Success

Declutter First

No organizer can fix an overstuffed closet. Be ruthless about what stays.

Measure Everything

Know your closet dimensions before buying anything. Returns are a hassle.

Start with One Zone

Don't buy everything at once. Add one organizer, live with it, then expand.

Rotate Seasonally

Store off-season clothes elsewhere (under bed, in bins). Keep only current season accessible.

Start Here

If you only do one thing, add a double hang rod. It takes 5 minutes to set up, costs under $20, and instantly doubles your hanging space. That single change makes everything else easier.
