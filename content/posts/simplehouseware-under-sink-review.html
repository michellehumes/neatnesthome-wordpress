Can I use this in a bathroom cabinet?

Absolutely! It works great for bathroom cabinets. The adjustable panels work around pedestal sink pipes just as well as kitchen plumbing.

SimpleHouseware rates it for up to 20 lbs per shelf. In our testing, it held full bottles of cleaning supplies without any bowing.
