Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept product samples for review?

We occasionally accept products for review, but we maintain full editorial control. We only publish reviews of products we believe will genuinely help our readers. Sending a product does not guarantee coverage.

Can I write a guest post for Neat Nest Home?

We're not currently accepting guest posts, but we appreciate your interest! Follow us on social media for any updates to this policy.

I found a broken link or error on your site. How do I report it?

Thank you for helping us improve! Please use the contact form above with "Website Feedback" as the subject and include the page URL where you found the issue.

We typically respond within 1-2 business days. For urgent matters, please note that in your message.
